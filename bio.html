Bio

Instructor, Martinos Center for Biomedical Imaging, Harvard Medical School

Research

My main interests are in using neuroimaging for various clinical applications. My current research focuses on the use of optically-pumped magnetometers (OPMs) for epilepsy. I use signal processing, machine learning, optimization, and 3D modeling for my research.
